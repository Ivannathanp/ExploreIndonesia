Fun Facts About Indonesia

The Republic of Indonesia, is an archipelago country, located off the coast of mainland Southeast Asia.
It consists of over 17,000 thousand islands, including Sumatra, Java, Sulawesi, and parts of Borneo and New Guinea, of which many are uninhabited. Hence, Indonesia is one of the world’s largest island country.

  • Capital: Jakarta
  • Official Language: Indonesian
  • Ethnic Groups: Over 300
  • Area: 1,916,907 sq km
  • Population: 270,203,917 (2020)
  • Currency: Indonesian rupiah (Rp)
